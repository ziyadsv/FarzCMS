Quality & Safety Policies

"Proudly following the footsteps of its founders that are based on commitment, dedication and customer satisfaction."

Quality Policy

Global Suhaimi Company (GSC) resolves to achieve and sustain a reputation for excellence in the Oil and Gas, Power Generation, Water Desalination, Petrochemicals industry in Saudi Arabia and the regional markets.

Global Suhaimi Company (GSC) is committed to:

  • Meet or exceed the expectations of customers.
  • Maintain efforts to show continual improvement in various activities aimed at achieving our Targets & Objectives.
  • Strive to satisfy the customer’s needs by executing the works to consistently high quality standards at competitive prices and within the timeframe agreed to.

This quality policy serves as a framework for evaluating quality objectives of different departments at various functional levels. It is ensured that these departmental objectives are consistent to this Quality Policy.

The highest level of efficiency within the company will back this quality commitment to assure the best possible services utilizing the latest available technologies, through continuous coordination with international scientific resources and employing a highly qualified work force.

Safety Policy

Global Suhaimi Company (GSC) is committed to provide and maintain a Safe and Healthy working Environment at all its Sites and prevent injuries and fatal accidents to its own personnel as well as Customers, Suppliers or other Organizations and damage or loss on any property by implementing adequate Safety Precautions and Practices”.

To fulfill above Safety Policy in achieving a better working environment, the guidelines to be followed by all Employees are indicated in a separate Procedure “Safety, Health And Environmental Requirements”.

To familiarize the requirements of this Documented Procedure and Safety Policy, Safety Officer or Site in Charge or Workshop in charge shall carryout a 10 minutes, Safety orientation at Sites every day before starting the activities.

To ensure that the Company fully meets the stated policy objectives, Executive Management subjects the effectiveness of the documented Safety Procedure to regular review. When conformity with any of the requirements of the Safety Procedure is impractical or extremely difficult, then an amendment to such requirement shall be initiated.

Any violation of Procedure Requirements reported by Client or Safety Officer or Site in Charge or Quality Auditors shall be dealt with strict disciplinary actions.

The company is seriously looking forward to acquire OHSAS 18001 and EMS- ISO 14001 certificates soon.
