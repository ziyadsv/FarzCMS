ABOUT GLOBAL SUHAIMI

In 1989 Al Suhaimi Group took full acquisition of Global Suhaimi’s shareholdings and reconstituted the Company to a 100% Saudi owned company to streamline the company operations with its corporate vision. By 2009 the manpower strength and operational plants capacity increased six folds compared with 1989. The Company was reorganized with strong and consistent commitment to provide highest quality services to customers with stringent project time scheduling.
